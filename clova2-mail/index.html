clova2Mail

clova2Mail is a Chrome extension that integrates CLOVA Note (Naver) with Gmail. It allows users to automatically compose and send Gmail emails using transcription results from CLOVA Note.

Features

  • Connect CLOVA Note transcription results directly to Gmail
  • Automatically compose emails from meeting notes and transcripts
  • Simple and easy-to-use Chrome extension

About This App

clova2Mail requests access to your Gmail account solely for the purpose of composing and sending emails based on your CLOVA Note transcription results. We do not store, share, or use your Gmail data for any other purpose.
